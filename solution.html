The Solution


You decide that you can make a change
You do not want buffelgrass to ruin your life anymore
You’ll show them
You do some research and find that there are various methods
of removing buffelgrass as a species
Since it is an invasive species
You believe that chemical control will be the most effective
after looking at all the options
You will use glyphosate, an herbicide that is relatively safe
for most plants and very effective for buffelgrass
Your home state of Arizona has 73 million acres and buffelgrass covers
about an eighth of the area in some way or another,
even if it may be sparse
For the herbicide, it takes about 2.5 quarts per acre
Factoring in the area, it takes about
5.7 million gallons of herbicide
For a highball estimate at the prices of amazon,
the costs of the herbicide
will be approximately 175 million dollars,
and you even get free shipping!
With only herbicide, it will just sit there,
so you need a crop duster to
Spread it for you, so you find one to do it
for about 15 dollars per acre
To bring you to a cost of 69 million dollars.
This is very expensive at 244 million dollars, so you
decide to go to the state of Arizona Department of Conservation
(1 year later)
The project for dusting herbicide
on the buffelgrass has been going great!
The project has about 1 year left of semi-annual dusting, but the
Herbicide proved to be very effective!
The Arizona state government got the stores selling buffel grass seeds
To also stop selling their stock in order to control the population
and promote local growth!
Your winner!
