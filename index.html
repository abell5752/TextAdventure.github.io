Welcome to Cactusville!


You are a cactus in southern Arizona just going about
your daily procedure of drinking water and absorbing
the sun to make food as a producer.
All the Bees around you are drinking your nectar from your flowers
And you feel great from this mutually beneficial
or symbiotic relationship
They take your nectar and get fed while
they pollinate you and spread your pollen
Without your best friend and keystone species, the bees,
you would die along with many other plants
And the effects would be catastrophic for the environment.
Many organisms going about their day are around you.
This interests you and you decide to investigate.
There are two animals in front of you.
These animals usually don’t bother you,
but they look really thirsty and willing to eat you.
Which one do you want to eat you?
The mouse or the rabbit?
