Vulture

You walk over to the road slowly and a big block is speeding towards you.
Everything goes black.
You awaken and you are a vulture eating your daily meal, freshly killed.
You are glad because now you are a scavenger
and all the food you eat is dead.
There are not many animals like you in the area
You don’t have to fight very hard for food because
you fill a specific niche in this environment
There are not many like you
You are the beginning of every end, and the end of time and space
You are vulture
And all roads lead to you
As you think this, you die unexpectedly
You crash down into the sand and there are beetles and millipedes around you
Which do you want to eat you?
